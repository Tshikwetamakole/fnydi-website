About Us

Far North Youth Development Initiative (FNYDI) is a registered NPO dedicated to empowering youth through education, creativity, and community-based programs.

Our Story

Founded in 2001, FNYDI has served thousands of young people across Limpopo through digital skills training, arts enrichment, and employment opportunities via EPWP.

Mission

To bridge the digital divide by fostering a tech-savvy, self-sufficient society through youth-centered learning and skills development.

Vision

We envision a future where every young person in our region has access to quality education, tools for innovation, and platforms for creative expression.
