Digital Skills Development

Free, industry-aligned IT training for underserved youth in partnership with Cisco, NEMISA, and University of Limpopo.

Our Goals

We aim to bridge the digital divide by empowering youth with tech skills that prepare them for real-world careers in the ICT sector.

Our Partners

Courses are accredited or endorsed by Cisco Networking Academy and supported by the NEMISA Digital Skills Initiative.

🎓 Register for Cisco Networking Academy

Courses Offered

  • 💻 Basic IT Literacy (MS Office, email, file management)
  • 📱 Mobile Digital Literacy - 1-day training short course by NEMISA, University of Limpopo - Limpopo Collab
  • 🌐 Networking Fundamentals
  • 💡 Coding Bootcamps (Python, JavaScript)
  • 🎨 Web & Graphic Design
  • ☁️ Google Workspace & Cloud Tools

Delivery Method

Training is delivered through a blended learning model — combining in-person workshops with online materials. Laptops, internet, and courseware are provided for onsite use.

Impact Stats

1,500+ youth trained since 2012. Completion rate: 99%
